OUR MISSION

We are a group of high school students who are passionate about teaching and helping others. Started in March 2020 to alleviate the stresses of COVID-19, ConneXions provides free ​tutoring services for students in grades K-12. We can help students struggling with homework as well as those seeking to get ahead of their classes. From schoolwork to standardized test preparation to even college readiness, we have tutors that accommodate a wide range of topics. Students/parents can sign up by clicking on our 'Find a Tutor' page and then selecting a meeting time with their preferred tutor.
