COLLEGE APPS SUPPORT

Our college advisors have been accepted to a wide variety of universities around the nation. They are passionate about helping high school seniors with their common app and supplemental essays, activities section, and scholarship applications. Each session is a 1 hour meeting with an advisor to receive help on any aspect of the college application process!
