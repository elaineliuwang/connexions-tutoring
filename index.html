OUR MISSION

ConneXions Tutoring started in March 2020 to alleviate the stresses of COVID-19. Our mission is to provide free ​tutoring services in all areas of study for students in grades K-12. From schoolwork to standardized test preparation to college application support, we have tutors and advisors that accommodate a wide range of topics. We have supported over 450 students and provided 2700+ hours of service.
